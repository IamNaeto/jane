About Me

I am Janefrancis Nginika Dimaku, a computer science graduate of Nnamdi Azikiwe University. I graduated at 2014 and served 2015/2016. I am a software engineer trained at ALX 2022/2023 session. Proficient in C, python and JavaScript programming languages. I am also hands on SQL, PHP, nginx, flask, APIs, react, tailwind css, Ubuntu and server management along with HTML5 and CSS3. Prior to studying at ALX, my passion in Information and communication technology has driven me to get a diploma in ICT. I have also worked as an IT support staff at different organizations which has given me hands on experience on system management and troubleshooting. I decided to focus on software engineering because of the zeal to explore its advancing world and to be able to build meaningful and useful applications for real life uses.
